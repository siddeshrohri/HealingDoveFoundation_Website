Project YES


Project YES(Youth for Emotional Support) concentrates on creating a chain of well trained individuals who can promote emotional wellbeing during times of emotional turmoil. Healing Dove Foundation works to help youth develop skills that enable them to manage their mental and emotional state effectively but also foster emotional first aid to their fellow companions. We would achieve these during our Workshops that focus on providing the beneficiaries with introspectiveness by concentrating on their true feelings and thoughts as well as centre around strengthening and deepening various sorts of relationships.
