Healing Dove Foundation Today

Where We Are




Now, the four-year-old organization is working with delinquents and various other marginalized groups such as specially-abled individuals, underprivileged women, underprivileged sportspeople, and the LGBTQ+ community, to name a few.




 

The aim is psychosocial, economic reformation and rehabilitation with the help of counselling, therapeutic activities, upskilling programs, and career development activities to the marginalized communities.


 


 

In a collaborative effort with ICHARS Support Foundation, Healing Dove Foundation launched Project Rainshine, Project MEHAK and Project YES.




 


 

They involve providing mental health assistance, capacity building programs, career-advancing endeavours, other training initiatives to youth, and organizations that are led by or working with youth.


 

What We Do?

 

We strive to work with mentors and trained practitioners, who work along with our beneficiaries to educate and spread knowledge and awareness about emotional and mental well being and adequate coping mechanisms by conducting valuable online sessions and various training programs in order to create a safe space for each and every participant in the hopes of sustainable and effective mental and emotional growth.

 
 

Our organisation concentrates into training the beneficiaries with imperative life skills, essential soft skills and exigent corporate skills by recruiting young individuals working in various sectors who will be trained to help and guide those who require skill development by various professionally-operated training sessions to ensure upliftment and provide social and economic rehabilitation and reformation for underprivileged youth.

 
 

Healing Dove Foundation helps to organise a youth-to-youth mentorship campaign in the hopes of providing the marginalised youth groups with adequate guidance and upliftment required for the career path the individual seeks. We assist our beneficiaries by guiding them for financial, induction, academic and industrial training in the hopes to help each and every individual develop holistically and become self-sufficient.
