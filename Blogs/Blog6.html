HOW PREPARED I AM AS A PRACTITIONER AFTER ATTENDING THIS TRAINING PROGRAM

- Moumita Paul. (Masters in Clinical Psychology)


Are you a psychology student?Are you looking for some productive learning? Do you want to gain some skills before working as a practitioner or professional??

If yes,

Then this article can surely help you.

One of the key experiences recommended as a student is doing an internship. Gaining work experience is key for boosting skills and knowledge.

Honestly speaking I undertook this internship blindly as during this pandemic time, I didn't have anything productive to do. So I thought of joining a foundation so that I can learn something and gain some experience.

And the cherry on top of the cake is the training session which I attended for this internship.

During this training program I got to learn many things about which I didn't have any idea. Each and every day I got to learn different things. The very first thing which I learned is about the talk out groups, how can i conduct a talk out group, what should be the flow, each and every detail. Then we were also explained about the survey part , support group, brand building and each and every point which are important for the psychology students in terms of building our career and also to settle ourselves in the field.

After this training program, I gained a new sense of professionalism and a clearer view of what it meant to be in the professional world. I entered the internship with the mindset that I will only be part of some usual activities but after attending this training I got to know about so many things. Started from how to write blogs to how to run a support group for people. I received guidance and great advice to help me move forward. To work as a practitioner I have gained much knowledge and skills . I would surely love to inculcate it . This training session was an eye opener to me and yes also a great add on both a professional and personal level. Now I can say that I am ready to take charge in this foundation and would give my best to each and every task.

This training provided each and every valuable information. Now I feel confident in applying this knowledge for the coming opportunities and to be a good practitioner.

I would love to Thank the Healing Dove foundation and Ichars for this very valuable training. I really enjoyed it, and I feel much better prepared .
